About HD Procurement

Built to connect agencies with the right delivery teams.

HD Procurement LLC is a small-business prime contractor focused on aligning government requirements with qualified subcontractors and vendors across the United States. Our structure allows us to operate efficiently while still delivering personal attention and responsive communication.

As owners, leadership is hands-on in every pursuit — from opportunity evaluation through subcontractor selection and project completion. We are intentional about the contracts we pursue so that we can show up fully for the agencies and communities we serve.

Why work with HD Procurement

We are designed for agencies and partners who value clarity, reliability, and a single accountable prime.

  • Registered business entity prepared for federal, state, and local work.
  • Prime contractor model with local subcontractor execution.
  • Direct access to decision-makers and project leads.
  • Technology-forward approach to opportunity discovery and tracking.
