Working together on federal bids

Our approach is straightforward: clarify the scope, manage the administrative work at each stage, and keep communication simple and documented.

1. Discovery & alignment

We start with a conversation to define the types of work you are interested in, the locations you prefer, and the timelines that make sense for your team.

  • Confirm core service areas and constraints
  • Review registrations and eligibility basics
  • Agree on communication channels and expectations

2. Monitoring & screening

We configure SAM.gov searches and related resources to identify opportunities that meet those criteria, then provide concise summaries for your review.

  • Regular monitoring during the business week
  • Short descriptions of key requirements
  • Clear due dates and action needed

3. Bid development

For approved opportunities, we organize the solicitation into a practical task list, support drafting, and coordinate with subcontractors where applicable.

  • Compliance checklists by document section
  • Drafting and formatting of response materials
  • Package assembly for electronic submission

4. After submission

Once a bid is submitted, we help track status, coordinate clarifications, and support the transition into performance if an award is made.

  • Maintain a summary of submitted bids
  • Organize clarifications or amendments
  • Assist with coordination as performance planning begins

Engagement options

We keep our structure simple so you always know what you are getting and how support is being provided.

Model Best for What HD Procurement handles
Per-opportunity support Businesses that want help on selected bids without a long-term commitment. Screening, checklisting, document preparation, and submission tasks for specific solicitations.
Ongoing monitoring Teams that want a consistent view of opportunities in their service area. Recurring SAM.gov monitoring, shortlists, and basic tracking of decisions.
Customized arrangement Agencies or primes with a unique volume of work or workflow. Tailored combination of monitoring, administration, and coordination tasks.
