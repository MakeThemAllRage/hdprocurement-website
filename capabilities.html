Core capabilities

HD Procurement LLC provides administrative and coordination services that allow field teams and subcontractors to focus on execution while we manage the documentation and communication that federal contracts require.

Opportunity research & alignment

We scan and filter opportunities based on scope, period of performance, and practical schedule alignment with your resources.

  • Daily monitoring of SAM.gov listings
  • Initial go/no-go review against your criteria
  • Summaries of requirements and due dates

Administrative contract support

From registration checks to documentation tracking, we manage the administrative side of federal work so your team can remain focused on operations.

  • Support with entity registrations and updates
  • Document control and version tracking
  • Basic reporting and status updates as requested

Subcontractor coordination

We help you assemble the right local performers for specific opportunities while maintaining a clearly defined scope for each project.

  • Assist with outreach to local firms
  • Coordinate quotes and written scopes
  • Track contact details and commitments
