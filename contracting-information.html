Contracting information

Key details for contracting officers and acquisition teams.

HD Procurement LLC is a coordination-focused prime contractor that supports multi-party work through clear procurement, subcontractor management, and administrative follow-through. This page brings together our core identifiers, NAICS / PSC alignment, and practical information for government decision-makers.

Engagement snapshot

  • Role: Prime contractor providing procurement and coordination support.
  • Service area: Nationwide, with work performed by qualified local subcontractors where services are delivered.
  • Response time: Within 24 hours for government inquiries.
  • Delivery model: Local licensed subcontractors and vendors managed under a single accountable prime.

How to engage

Simple starting point for pre-award conversations.

A short email with the anticipated scope, locations, and any known NAICS or PSC codes is often enough to determine whether HD Procurement LLC is a good fit for an upcoming requirement.

  1. Share a brief description of the requirement and any known NAICS / PSC.
  2. Note performance locations and timelines so we can confirm subcontractor availability.
  3. Identify the role – prime, subcontractor, or teaming partner.
  4. We respond within 24 hours with follow-up questions or a short capability summary.
