Services

Federal procurement and project support, from bid to closeout.

HD Procurement LLC is structured to serve as the prime contractor, coordinating local subcontractors, suppliers, and professionals to meet government contract requirements efficiently and reliably.

Prime contracting & bid management

Identification, evaluation, and pursuit of opportunities, with a focus on contracts where our team and partners can deliver strong performance.

Subcontractor & vendor sourcing

Sourcing and vetting of local subcontractors and vendors who meet licensing, insurance, and capability requirements for each contract.

Contract administration

Coordination of communications, documentation, and reporting to keep agencies, primes, and subs aligned with requirements and timelines.

Opportunity pipeline management

Internally, we are building an AI-assisted engine to monitor SAM.gov and other sources for high-fit opportunities that align with our capabilities.

Partner & teaming support

Flexible teaming arrangements with other primes and partners where shared capabilities and past performance create a stronger offer.

Scope & performance oversight

Monitoring of work progress and quality through our subcontractors, with a focus on meeting scope, schedule, and safety expectations.
