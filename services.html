Services

Prime contracting and coordination built around clear responsibility.

HD Procurement LLC is structured to serve as the accountable prime on projects that involve more than one firm. Our services focus on planning, sourcing, and day-to-day coordination so owners, agencies, and partners have a single point of responsibility.

Prime contracting & project oversight

Acting as the prime, we lead contracts and expectations so there is a clear “owner” of the work being performed, even when several companies are involved.

Subcontractor & vendor sourcing

We look for qualified local companies that match the technical needs, scale, and schedule of each project, then bring them into a coordinated plan with defined roles and expectations.

Procurement coordination

We help organize the purchasing of services and materials so requirements, timelines, and responsibilities are understood by everyone involved.

Administrative support

We assist with documentation, basic reporting, and communication tasks that support progress and keep decision-makers informed.

Multi-trade project support

For projects that combine several trades or vendors, we focus on keeping scope, schedules, and communication aligned under one accountable prime.

Partner & teaming support

We are open to teaming with other firms where shared capabilities and aligned approaches create a stronger offer for the client or agency.

Working with government agencies

For contracting officers and acquisition staff, detailed identifiers, NAICS / PSC alignment, and additional government-specific information are available on our dedicated Government page.
