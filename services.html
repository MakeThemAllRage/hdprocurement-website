Services

Prime contracting and procurement built around coordination.

HD Procurement LLC is structured to serve as the prime contractor and central coordination point for projects that involve multiple subcontractors and vendors. Even as we begin building our portfolio, our focus remains the same: reliable organization and clear responsibility.

Prime contracting & project oversight

Acting as the prime, we lead contracts and expectations, providing a single responsible party for organizing work and communicating with stakeholders.

Subcontractor & vendor sourcing

We identify and coordinate with local subcontractors and vendors whose capabilities, scale, and availability align with each project’s needs.

Procurement coordination

From basic materials and services to specialized support, we help organize procurement activities so requirements and timelines remain clear.

Administrative support

We assist with documentation, communication, and other administrative tasks that support smooth execution throughout the project lifecycle.

Partner & teaming support

We are open to collaborating with other firms where shared capabilities and aligned approaches create a stronger delivery team.

Flexible scope & sector support

Our model is designed to adapt to different sectors and project types, with a consistent emphasis on coordination and accountability.
