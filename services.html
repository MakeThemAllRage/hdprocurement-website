Services

Prime contracting and procurement built around coordination.

HD Procurement LLC is structured to serve as the prime contractor and central coordination point on projects that involve more than one firm. Our services focus on the planning, sourcing, and organization needed to support reliable delivery.

Prime contracting & project oversight

Acting as the prime, we lead contracts and expectations and provide a single responsible party for organizing work and communicating with clients.

Subcontractor & vendor sourcing

We look for qualified local companies that match the technical needs, scale, and schedule of each project, then bring them into a coordinated plan.

Procurement coordination

We help organize the purchasing of services and materials so requirements and timing are understood by everyone involved.

Administrative support

We assist with documentation, basic reporting, and communication tasks that support day-to-day progress.

Partner & teaming support

We are open to teaming with other firms where shared capabilities and aligned approaches create a stronger offer.

Flexible scope & sector support

Our model is designed to adapt to different sectors and project types while keeping the same emphasis on clarity and coordination.
