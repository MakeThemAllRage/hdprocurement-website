For contracting officers

Information for acquisition and program teams.

HD Procurement LLC is a coordination-focused prime contractor that supports multi-party work through clear procurement, subcontractor management, and administrative follow-through. This page is designed to give contracting officers and acquisition staff the key information needed for market research, pre-award conversations, and potential solicitations.

Engagement snapshot

  • Response time: Within 24 hours for government inquiries.
  • Service area: Nationwide, with work performed by qualified local subcontractors where services are delivered.
  • Role: Prime contractor providing procurement, coordination, and administrative support.
  • Delivery model: Local licensed subcontractors and vendors, managed under a single accountable prime.

When to consider HD Procurement

  • Projects where multiple trades or vendors must be coordinated under a single point of responsibility.
  • Requirements that benefit from clear administrative support, documentation, and schedule tracking.
  • Needs for local performance in different regions while maintaining consistent oversight.
  • Teaming or small-business participation strategies that rely on structured subcontractor management.

How to engage

Simple steps for pre-award conversations.

We aim to make it easy for acquisition and program staff to understand where HD Procurement LLC fits into upcoming work. A short email with the anticipated scope, NAICS, and performance location is often enough to start a productive discussion.

  1. Share a brief description of the requirement and any known NAICS or PSC codes.
  2. Note locations and timelines so we can confirm subcontractor availability.
  3. Identify how HD Procurement might help – as a prime, subcontractor, or teaming partner.
  4. We respond within 24 hours with follow-up questions or a short capability summary.
