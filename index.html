What we do

Procurement, contracting, and coordinated project support.

HD Procurement LLC is built to serve as the primary point of accountability on projects involving multiple subcontractors or vendors. We align the right partners, manage communication, and maintain structure throughout the project lifecycle.

Prime contracting & oversight

We lead the contracting process, establish clear expectations, and coordinate all contributing parties to ensure work stays aligned with scope and standards.

Subcontractor & vendor sourcing

We identify capable local partners, verify qualifications, and ensure they are aligned with project goals, timelines, and requirements.

Procurement & administrative support

From documentation and planning to ongoing communication, we help keep the process organized and expectations clear for all stakeholders.
