What we do

Procurement, coordination, and delivery — under one accountable prime.

HD Procurement LLC is structured to act as the prime contractor, sourcing and managing qualified subcontractors close to the project site. That means agencies get a single point of contact and a team built for the job.

Prime contracting & procurement

We pursue and manage public sector contracts, aligning requirements with the right subcontractors and suppliers while maintaining schedule, scope, and compliance.

Subcontractor sourcing & vetting

We identify qualified local partners, confirm licensing and insurance, and ensure they understand the project and performance expectations.

Administrative & compliance support

From documentation to reporting and communication, we keep stakeholders informed and paperwork aligned with agency requirements.
