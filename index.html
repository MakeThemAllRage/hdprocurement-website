What we help you manage

Whether you’re new to federal work or expanding an existing portfolio, we handle the day-to-day effort of sourcing, coordinating, and submitting targeted opportunities.

SAM.gov opportunity monitoring

We configure and maintain searches that align with your qualified scope, monitoring for new solicitations, RFQs, RFIs, and pre-solicitations on a recurring basis.

  • Set up and refine saved searches
  • Sort by practicable timelines and scope fit
  • Provide concise digests of eligible opportunities

Bid package development

Our team organizes each requirement, assembles supporting documentation, and coordinates with you and your teaming partners to submit a responsive bid.

  • Compliance checklists by section and attachment
  • Drafting and formatting of proposal documents
  • Submission support through the designated portal

Prime–subcontractor coordination

Work can be performed anywhere in the United States by engaging qualified local subcontractors while we remain your central coordination point.

  • Assist in identifying local partners for performance
  • Coordinate scopes of work and pricing inputs
  • Maintain communication channels during performance
